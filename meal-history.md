# Meal history

A rolling record of what was on each week, so the weekly plan can avoid repeating a batch lunch dish from the last two weeks, rotate the protein base, keep oily fish to once or twice, and track new foods. Newest week first. The weekly run reads the top two entries before planning and adds a new entry at the end of each run.

Each entry records: week commencing, the Sunday batch cook, the childminder (batch) lunch dishes, the proteins used, the oily fish, the new foods introduced, and any notes.
